Guidelines to join:

  • Weekly mileage check-in: Log your miles/steps to your team tally or to the "all-members total"
  • Stay in touch: Communicate and coordinate with other members using our app, available online or as a downloadable app for your smartphone
  • Provide a phone number or e-mail: So we can send reminders to run, and alert you when other members are looking for someone to tackle a specific goal with!
