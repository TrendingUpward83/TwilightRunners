About Twilight Runners

We are a group of people who are committed to getting up, getting out and being on the move. Whether it's a walk on a treadmill at a gym, a few laps around the yard or a full marathon, every step taken each day counts. A few of us run on local trails together, many of us log our miles independently, some run fast and others slow. What we have in common is that we aim to get the majority of our steps in during twilight- those wee hours in the morning or sometime around dusk.

Whether you are completely new to exercise or you have a few Ironmans under your belt, there's a spot for you here. You are free to join up with others at your level, create in-person groups or have online only mileage challenges, look to one another for inspiration, share tips and encourage one another.

Remember, you don't have to go fast, you just have to go!
